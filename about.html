Our Journey

From a small startup to Kenya's premier design and property firm

Founded in 2010

Property & Design began as a passion project by founder Jane Kamau, who saw the need for integrated design and real estate services in Kenya. What started as a small design studio has grown into a multidisciplinary firm serving clients across East Africa.

Our journey has been marked by innovation, dedication, and a commitment to excellence. We've transformed over 500 spaces and helped more than 1,000 clients find their dream properties.

Today, we're proud to be a team of 30+ professionals, including interior designers, architects, real estate experts, and project managers, all working together to deliver exceptional results.
