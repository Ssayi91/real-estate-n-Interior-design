Affordable Housing Initiative

At Kenya Design & Estates, we're addressing Kenya's housing deficit with quality, affordable solutions. Our core focus is developing modern two-bedroom bungalows and townhouses that combine functionality, style, and affordability.

Inspired by Kenya's commitment to housing for all, we're creating communities where families can thrive without compromising on quality or design.
