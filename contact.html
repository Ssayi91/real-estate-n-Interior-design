Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common inquiries about our services

How soon can I expect a response after submitting the contact form?

Our team typically responds within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent inquiries, we recommend calling our office directly or contacting us via WhatsApp for a faster response.

Do you offer virtual consultations for international clients?

Yes, we offer comprehensive virtual consultations via Zoom, Microsoft Teams, or WhatsApp video calls. These sessions typically last 60-90 minutes and include a detailed discussion of your project requirements, design preferences, and budget considerations.

What areas in Kenya do you serve for real estate services?

We currently serve clients in Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and surrounding areas. For properties outside these regions, we offer specialized consultation services and can coordinate with local partners.

What is the typical timeline for an interior design project?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A standard residential project takes 4-8 weeks from concept to completion, while commercial projects may take 8-16 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
